Hover Car could go underwater and fly to space. It could help Li Hui and Hu Jie go underwater in the city river to discover and stop villains.

Hover Car offers many awesome features and abilities for Li Hui and Hu Jie. All the features are listed below:

• Flying: Hover Car could fly around Perle, bringing Li Hui and Hu Jie to any place they want.
• Submarine: Hover Car could also go underwater like a submarine. The Hover Car could bring Li Hui and Hu Jie into the deepest place underwater.
• Speed: Hover Car could go super fast in the sky, underwater, and on the road.]
